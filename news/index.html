New Grant Opportunity for CACFP Modernization

‹ Back to CNPSuite

Attention State Agencies:

Under the leadership of Secretary Brooke Rollins, the USDA is committed to supporting state-driven innovations that strengthen program integrity and improve the delivery of nutrition assistance.

To advance these priorities, the Food and Nutrition Administration (FNA) has announced the availability of $8.7 million for the FY 2026 CACFP Integrity Technology Innovation Grants (TIG).

What the Grant Supports

These funds are designed to help State agencies design and implement modernized information technology systems. Supported activities include:


  • Developing statewide CACFP systems.

  • Creating interfaces with sponsoring organization systems.

  • Enhancing automated edit checks and reporting.

  • Supporting local-level automation through subgrants.

Key Grant Details

  • Total Funding: $8.7 Million

  • Award Size: 5–10 competitive grants ranging from $100,000 to $2 million.


Timeline: Performance period runs from September 2026 through September 2029.


Application Deadline: Friday, July 31, 2026, at 11:59 PM EDT.



How to Apply

The Request for Applications (RFA) and all supporting document templates (including application and budget narratives) are now available on Grants.gov.

  • Visit Grants.gov and click “Get Started.”

  • Select “Search for Opportunity Package.”

  • Enter Funding Opportunity number: [USDA-FNA-CN-TIG-2026]
