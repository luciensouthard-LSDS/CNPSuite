About this deck. Written from the state agency’s seat: each card covers what a federal reviewer probes about SA administration of the Summer Food Service Program, drafted from 7 CFR Part 225, FNS Instruction 796-4 Rev. 4, FNS Instruction 113-1, and the 2022 SFSP integrity final rule. As of June 1, 2026, FNS is the Food and Nutrition Administration (FNA), so newer source documents may carry the new name. This is a study aid — confirm every item against your FNA regional office’s ME guidance, the current eCFR, and your agency’s own procedures before relying on it in a review.
